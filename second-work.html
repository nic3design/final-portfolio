Josef Müller-Brockmann Booklet

This is a booklet I created about the methods and principles of the legendary graphic designer Josef Müller-Brockmann. Here I showcased his work and outlined key elements of his designs that have laid a foundation for all graphic designers.

I then created 4 posters within the book using his methods of design.
