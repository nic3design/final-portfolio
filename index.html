Who I Am

Graphic Designer. Visual Communicator. Art Enthusiast.

My name is Nicole and I am a graphic designer in Austin, Texas. I am greatly passionate about what I do and I enjoy the challenges this industry gives me. I appreciate the embrace of arts and technology equally, and aim to solve the problems that each unique client has to offer.

I am very passionate about life and the creativity of people. I feel that life is a gift and I intend to get as much out of it as I can by using my talents and abilities to the fullest extent. I strive to share joy and inspire other people through visual communication. I want to use graphic design to make the world a more beautiful place.

What I Do

I specialize in Graphic Design, Visual Design and Web Design.

I've done extensive work with graphic design starting with the foundational principles of design. I apply the best known methods to ensure a successful design by using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

I use graphic design principles to code in HTML & CSS by following the guides of grids and hierarchy. I have built web pages by making templates in Adobe XD and building them with Sublime.

I've used various mediums of illustration including pen & ink, pencil, charcoal, and painting. I've also created digital illustrations using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
