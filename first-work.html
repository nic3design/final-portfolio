MakeCrate Visual Design Internship

About MakeCrate

MakeCrate is a monthly subscription package provider for children and teens. Each month the customer is provided with the hardware and software instructions to build small tech projects like alarm clocks, Simon says games, mini robots, and much more. The projects are comprised of two parts- build and code. The projects are like mini games and the user is rewarded upon completion of each course.

I worked for a semester as an intern for them and primarily did visual design for the courses and for the promotional advertising.

My Job

I was in charge of coming up with a system for the courses. The build and coding were divided into easy, medium, and hard. I devised a way to easily differentiate between those. I also came up with a color for each course while trying to not deviate too far from the bright logo colors.
